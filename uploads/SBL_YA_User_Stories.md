**SBL Young Ambassador Platform**

* Young Ambassador Applicant: a student or young professional discovering the program and applying.

* Active Young Ambassador: a selected participant using the platform to contribute, track progress, and earn recognition.

* General Visitor: a founder, mentor, investor, or diaspora member browsing the platform without registering.

* SBL Admin: internal SBL staff managing applications, scoring, and platform content.

#  Young Ambassador  Applicant 

 As a prospective applicant who clicks on the website link, they will land on the YA Platform and will view SBL's mission and the ambassador program overview on the homepage, so that they understand the program's purpose and journey before applying.

* Hero section displays the program name, tagline, and a clear call to action.

* Scrolling reveals SBL's vision and program impact statistics as animated counters  ***(Gamification)***

* An interactive world map displays a pin for each country with an active or past ambassador  ***(Gamification)***

* A "How It Works" section outlines the ambassador journey: Apply → Select → Contribute → Reward

* Sign In and Sign Up buttons are persistently visible in the navigation bar

###  **Register / Apply**

A prospective applicant has to register through a sign-up form so that they can become a Young Ambassador.

* Clicking Sign Up opens an in-page registration form without navigating away from the homepage

* A sign up form with username and password shows up which needs to be filled. Then after clicking Next

  A new form captures: Full Name, Age, Phone, Email, Gender, University/Institution, Academic Year, CGPA, Current City/ Country of Residence, Nationality, Achievements, and CV upload.

* Required fields needs to be filled  CV upload should be in pdf/docx, max file size is enforced.

* On submission, the applicant receives on-screen confirmation over email


### **Track Application Status**

As an applicant, anyone would  want to track their application status after submission, so that they know where they stand in the selection process.

* Applicant can log in with username and given password and view a status tracker: Pending → Under Review → Shortlisted → Selected / Not Selected

* There will be a profile dashboard to see the information they have provided

* Status changes trigger email and notifications in the website

* Once selected, the submitted country of residence registers a new pin on the world map is marked ***(Gamification)***

# Active Young Ambassador

 As a newly selected ambassador, I want a guided onboarding flow, so that I understand my role and complete required setup steps.

* Welcome message on first login

* Checklist pr % sign to show profile completion, Code of Conduct acknowledgment, and joining the ambassador community channel (whatsapp)

* Completing onboarding unlocks full dashboard access and awards a starter badge  ***(Gamification)***

### **Dashboard Overview**

As an active ambassador, each member has a personal dashboard so that they can track their progress and activity at a glance.

* Profile card displays name, university, and current rank/level (Bronze / Silver / Gold / Regional Lead)  ***(Gamification)***

* XP/points progress bar shows proximity to the next rank  ***(Gamification)***

* Streak counter displays consecutive months of active participation  ***(Gamification)***

* Badge shelf displays all unlocked achievement badges  ***(Gamification)*** 

* World map view shows the ambassador's own location, visually highlighted against other pins  ***(Gamification)***

### **US-06  Log Contributions**

An active ambassador will have a log of their activities and contributions so that they are tracked, verified, and scored.

* "Log Activity" form captures activity type, description, supporting evidence/attachment, and impact notes or description

* Submitted activities enter "Pending Review" status

* Approved activities are scored on Output and Impact using the defined High (8–10) / Medium (4–7) / Low (1–3) scale

* Approved activities award points toward the ambassador's XP bar and may unlock badges  ***(Gamification)***

* Approval triggers an in-app confirmation notification and uploads to the Opportunity/ news feed of the platform

###  **Leaderboard**

**A**n active ambassador will view a leaderboard, so that they can see their  standing relative to other ambassadors.

* Leaderboard is available in both a ranked list view  ***(Gamification)***

* Filters are available for time period (monthly/all-time), region, and university

* The logged-in ambassador's own rank is highlighted, with a "points to next rank" indicator displayed  ***(Gamification)***

* Rank changes triggers a personal notification  ***(Gamification)***

###  **Rewards ( needs to be discussed)**

**A**n active ambassador will  view available and unlocked rewards, so that I understand the benefits tied to my performance.

* Rewards tab lists benefits already unlocked at the ambassador's current rank (recognition, event access, mentorship opportunities)  ***(Gamification)***

* Both extrinsic incentives (certificates, monetary/non-monetary rewards) and intrinsic incentives (skill development, leadership titles) are represented

* Next-tier rewards and the criteria to unlock them are clearly displayed  ***(Gamification)***


# General Visitor

### **Public Discovery**

A  general visitor (founder, mentor, student, or diaspora member) want to browse the platform without registering, so that they can discover ambassadors, opportunities, and SBL's mission.

* Public world map displays ambassador density per country, viewable without login  ***(Gamification)***

* Hovering or clicking a country reveals active ambassador count and featured stories

* Opportunity/news feed is browsable and searchable 

* About and Contact pages are accessible without registration

# SBL Admin

### **Application Review**

A SBL Admin will review and score applications, so that they can select qualified ambassadors efficiently.

* Sortable and filterable applicant table displays all registration fields, including CGPA, achievements, and CV

* Scoring the achievements system that supports the merit-based evaluation framework we created

* One-click approve/reject action triggers automated applicant notification

* Approval automatically activates the applicant's pin on the ambassador map  ***(Gamification)***

### **Activity & Leaderboard Management**

A SBL Admin would want to review logged activities and manage the leaderboard, so that scoring remains accurate and fair.

* Admin can approve or reject logged activities with Output/Impact scoring

* Approved activities automatically update ambassador points, rank, and map badge status  ***(Gamification)***

# Registration Field Reference

The following fields are collected at application. 

* Full Name

* Age

* Gender

* University / Institution

* Academic Year

* CGPA

* Current City & Country of Residence — powers the world map pin

* Nationality — distinguishes Bangladeshi nationals abroad (NRB) for eligibility and reporting

* Achievements (free text / tags)

* CV (PDF upload)
